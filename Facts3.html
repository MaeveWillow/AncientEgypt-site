Pyramids were used as tombs for Pharos.

When Pharos died, they were mummified
and placed into coffins. Those coffins
would then be placed into Pyramids,
and the Pharo's coffins would be
surrounded by gold and riches so that
they could make it into the afterlife.
Sadly many tombs have been raided and
robbed, so most pyramids that we've
been able to enter are empty.
